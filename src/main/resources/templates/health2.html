The most popular diseases

Here are the most popular diseases and their symptoms, if applicable.

Influenza (Flu)

Symptoms include fever, cough, sore throat, runny or stuffy nose, body aches, headache, chills, and fatigue.

Diabetes

Symptoms include increased thirst and hunger, frequent urination, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, blurred vision, and slow-healing sores or frequent infections.

Asthma

Symptoms include wheezing, coughing, shortness of breath, and chest tightness.

Hypertension (High blood pressure)

Symptoms may not be noticeable, but over time, high blood pressure can cause health problems such as heart disease, stroke, and kidney failure. It is often detected during a routine checkup or screening test.
